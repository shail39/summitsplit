About SummitSplit

SummitSplit is a free expense-splitting app for group trips. Create trips, add members, track expenses, and settle balances — all for free with no subscriptions or in-app purchases.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a trip?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, enter a trip name, select a currency and icon, then tap Create.

How do I add members?

Open a trip and go to the Members tab. Tap "Add Member" to add people to your trip.

How do I split an expense?

In a trip, tap "Add Expense". Enter the amount, who paid, and choose how to split: equally, by exact amounts, shares, or percentages.

How do I settle balances?

Go to the Balances tab in your trip to see who owes whom and the suggested settlements.

Is SummitSplit free?

Yes, SummitSplit is completely free. There are no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no ads.

How is my data handled?

Your name and email are stored locally on your device. Trip and expense data is stored on our server to enable sharing trips with others.
